Aladdin

Name: Aladdin

Age: 18

Description: He has short black hair, brown eyes and he is quite muscular. He wears a small purple fez, a purple waistcoat and cream baggy trousers with a yellow patch on the right side.

Background: Aladdin was born to Cassim (The King of Thieves) and an unnamed woman. He lives in a broken city called Agrabah.

Personality: He is kind, caring, sweet, brave, attractive. He is also loving to Jasmine and his pet monkey Abu. He is lso a very agile and an infamous person.
