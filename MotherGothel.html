Mother Gothel

Name: Mother Gothel

Age: over 400 years old

Description: She wears full length wine red dress with gold lining and a brown rope belt. She has black curly hair that comes to just below her shoulders and her eyes are a mix of grey and blue.

Background: She was an elderly woman near the end of her life until she witnessed a single drop of pure sunlight fall to the ground creating a magical flower with magic healing abilities. When she sings to it and strokes its petals, she becomes young and beautiful. She tries to keep the flower a secret from the rest of the world but fails when the queen of a nearby kingdom falls deathly ill while pregnant hundreds of years later.

Personality: She presents herself with a theatrical flair, often flaunting her beauty and curvaceous appearance with enthusiasm and dramatics. She is a phenominal actress, a manipulator and she is very intelligent.
