Princess Jasmine

Name: Jasmine

Age: 15

Despription: She has long black hair that she keeps together with blue ribbons and she has brown eyes. She has a blue head piece that has a centrepiece that is a shiny blue gem. She wears a bronze necklace and triangle earrings. She also wears a blue, off the shoulder crop top with blue baggy bottoms, similar to Aladdins. She also wears dainty, blue lamp shoes.

Background: Her father is the Sultan of Agrabah, who is actually called Hamed. Her mother died during her childhood. She has a pet tiger called Rajah who she got when she was young.She has always been trapped in her castle and had always dreamed of seeing a whole new world.

Personality: She is a stubborn person and she knows right from wrong. She wants to marry for love and she loves adventuring with Aladdin. She is a lovable person and very pretty.
