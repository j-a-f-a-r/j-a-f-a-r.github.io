Flynn

Name: Flynn Rider(Eugene Fitzherbert)

Age: 26

Description: He has short brown hair with brown eyes and also has a goatee. He wears a white, long sleeved shirt and a blue cow leather vest. He wears biege trousers and a brown belt that has a small bag. He also wears dark brown pirate boots.

Background: Flynn(Eugene) never knew his parents and spent most of the time in the orphanage. He told the other orphans tales of 'The Tales of Flynnigan Rider' a rich and dashing swashbuckler. Once he left the orphanage when he was older, he went by the alias Flynn Rider.

Personality: To begin with, he's arrogant, backstabbing and a trouble-maker. Although his arrogance is more of a defence mechanism and it provides a false sense of optimism when in reality he's vunerable and capable of showing and feeling legititmate emotions. His facade is dropped when he opens up to Rapunzel and becomes a loving, loyal and protective companion.
